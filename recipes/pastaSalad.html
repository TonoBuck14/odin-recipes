Pasta Salad


Description

This dish is an Italian delight that's very easy to prepare and has a wonderful taste.


Ingredients
  • 1 package fusilli pasta
  • 3 cups of cherry tomatoes
  • 1/2 pound provolone cheese
  • 1/2 pound salami
  • 1/4 pound sliced pepperoni
  • 1 large green bell pepper
  • 1 can black olives
  • 1 jar pimentos
  • 1 bottle italian salad dressing
Steps
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook fusilli pasta int the boiling water stirring occasionally, until tender, about 12 minutes. Drain
  3. Combine all the ingredients
  4. Enjoy.
