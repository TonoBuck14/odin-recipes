Chimichurri Sauce


Description

This dish is an argentinian sauce that goes very well with all kinds of meats


Ingredients
  • 1 cup fresh parsley
  • 3/4 cup extra virgin oil
  • 3 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tbsp dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons pepper sauce
  • 1 teaspoon salt
Steps
  1. Combine parsley, oil, vinegar, oregano, cumin, garlic, hot sauce, and salted in a blender. Mix on medium speed until ingredients are evenly blended, about 10 seconds
